Who We Are

Fly Share, a groundbreaking luggage-sharing app, revolutionizes how passengers manage baggage during flights. Connecting travelers on the same flight, it facilitates the exchange of excess luggage space, fostering collaboration and cost-saving. Dedicated to reshaping air travel, Fly Share sees it as an opportunity for community-building. Join us for a more connected and efficient travel experience.

Our Vision

Fly Share's vision is to transform air travel through a global community, seamlessly connecting passengers to share excess luggage space and fostering collaboration. Our Mission

Fly Share's mission is to revolutionize travel through community-driven luggage sharing on flights. The aim is to connect passengers with excess luggage space, fostering collaboration, optimizing travel expenses, and reducing carbon footprints. By facilitating luggage space sharing, the mission empowers travelers to save money, unlock new experiences, and build meaningful connections while promoting efficient and eco-friendly flying.
